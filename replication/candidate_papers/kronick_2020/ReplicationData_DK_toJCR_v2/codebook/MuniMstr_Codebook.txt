This codebook lists the definitions of variables included in the municipality-level panel data set used in “Profits and Violence in Illegal Markets: Evidence from Venezuela” (Kronick 2020).


estado: Name of the state


municipio: Name of the municipality


ci_mun: Numeric ID of the municipality. Note that (as in Mexico) these are not the same as the numeric IDs used by the electoral institute.


year: Calendar year


pob_cgr: Municipal population; constant growth rate interpolation from decennial census counts


ln_pob: ln(pob_cgr). Note, there are no observations with population zero.


pob_1990: ln(municipal population in 1990)


hrate_combo: Violent deaths per 100,000 population. See Appendix A of the paper for details on the source and the definition.


ln_hrate_combo: ln(1+hrate_combo)


M1year_rate_combo: Deaths of children under one year per 100,000 population


srate_combo: Suicides per 100,000 population


panam_sucre: Indicator for whether a municipality is adjacent to the Panamerican Highway between the Colombian border and Sucre state. See map in Appendix F.


panam_neighbors: Indicator for municipalities adjacent to the municipalities in panam_sucre


treat_panam_sucre: panam_sucre interacted with an indicator for years >= 1989 


pol_local: Indicator for presence of municipal police


alc_oficialista: Mayor is pro-Chavez


hrate_demean: violent death rate, de-meaned by municipality 


m1year_demean: m1year_rate_combo, de-meaned by municipality
